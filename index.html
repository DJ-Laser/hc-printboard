PrintBoard

A Hackclub YSWS where you ship a 3D design that interfaces with the Ikea Pegboard, and we will send you one!

What is the Ikea Pegboard?

The Ikea Skadis is a 30 by 22 inch wooden board with many holes for you to connect your designs to the wall. It is often used by 3D Modelers and hackers to easily connect prints to the wall without needing screws or adhesives.

What are the requirements

  1. Your submisson must be unique. If a similar 3d model already exists (check on sites like thingiverse) you will be asked to clarify what makes yours better
  2. Your submisson must have a purpose and solve a problem.
  3. You must be 18 or under.

Ready to start?

Great! If you already know how to use CAD, just make a new project in a editor of your choice, and get started! If you do not know how to use CAD, Click here for a Guide to get started!
