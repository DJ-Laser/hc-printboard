TinkerCAD Guide

Step One:

Open up TinkerCAD and log in or sign up to a account (Make sure to select personal account and not school) then create a new "3D Design".

Step Two:

Drag in a oval, then rotate it 90Â° towards the ground so it is flat to the ground, then click the arrows and set the dimensions to what is shown in the image below.

Step Three:

Click on the small down arrow on the "Cylinder" window, and then change the sides to 50 to make it as smooth as possible.

Step Four:

Click where it says basic shapes then switch it to design starters, then click the "A" to switch to letters and numbers.

Step Five:

Click "More Shapes" until you see the letter J, then drag it onto the board.

Step Six:

Rotate the J 180 degrees horizontally, and scale it to match the dimensions in the image.

Step Seven:

Drag the J over to the cylinder you created earlier to match the image.

Congrats! You have created a basic hook that can attach to the Ikea Skadis. you may now add onto this however you want to build your project!
